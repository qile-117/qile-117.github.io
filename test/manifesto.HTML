Manifesto

Good image

High quality, high resolution

Algorithmically enhanced

Composed according to aesthetic conventions

Well-lit

Subjects are always front-facing

Free of clutter or distracting objects

No one is blinking in group photos

Smiling

No prohibited or inappropriate items within the frame

No motion blur

Clean camera lens

Taken at recognizable landmarks

Higher degree of skin exposure

More sexually appealing

Anthropocentric in focus

Easy to identify

Easy to categorize

Poor image

All that fails to meet the standards of good image

Captured in haste, by accident, or without purpose

Blurry, dark, overexposed, or underexposed

Offline, untagged, and untracked

Cropped, compressed, corrupted, pixelated

Raw and unprocessed

Natural

Real and unperformed

Difficult to classify and recognize

Nonhuman photography
